CycleStay

Trade your apartment, not your savings.

Every summer, tens of thousands of students relocate for internships and face the same problem simultaneously: they need to sublet their place and find short-term housing in a new city. CycleStay uses cycle-finding matching — structurally identical to the algorithm behind kidney-exchange programs — to identify 3- and 4-way swaps no bilateral platform can find.

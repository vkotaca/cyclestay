How it works

Three steps to your summer home

01 — List

Post your place in 5 minutes

Add your apartment details, available dates, and move preferences. No listing fees, no subscriptions. Your home stays visible until a cycle is confirmed.

02 — Match

Our algorithm finds your cycle

We run kidney-exchange-style cycle matching across the network. You're notified the moment a 2-, 3-, or 4-way swap is found that fits everyone's dates and preferences.

Questions

Frequently asked

What if someone drops out of my cycle?
The matching engine attempts automatic repair by substituting a verified standby participant. If no substitute is available, you are released from the cycle and re-entered into the next run at priority status — no penalty, no lost time.
Do I exchange money with the other students?
No. The swap is rent-free — you live in their place while they live in yours (or in someone else's in the cycle). You pay only a small coordination fee to CycleStay to cover matching, verification, and support. No money moves between participants.
How are participants verified?
All participants verify a university email address. We also collect LinkedIn profile links and optional letters of good standing from housing offices. The network spans MBA and graduate programs across multiple universities — cycles only work when many schools' summer flows intersect.
What unit types are supported?
Studios, 1BRs, 2BRs, and private rooms in shared apartments. The matching algorithm weighs unit type compatibility as part of its score — you specify what you need on the receiving end, and you'll only be placed in cycles where preferences align.
Can I see the matcher in action?
Yes — click "Launch matcher" in the nav or "Find my match" above. The live demo runs the full cycle-finding algorithm on synthetic student profiles and shows match rates, cycle visualizations, and a sensitivity sweep.

The fix

Don't trade with one stranger. Cycle with three.

CycleStay treats summer housing as a network problem. We close 3- and 4-way loops: A's apartment goes to B, B's to C, C's to A. Everyone moves once. Nobody pays twice.

Same trust model as a 1-for-1 swap (verified students, university email), but with orders of magnitude more matches because the algorithm finds chains, not pairs.
